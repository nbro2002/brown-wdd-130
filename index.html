COVID-19 SAFETY PROTOCOLS

  1. No lines or lobbies! Our lobby is now closed. Please wait in your car and text/call us when you arrive for your appointment. We will call or text you when we are ready to bring you into the office.
  2. Halloween came early this year. All Patients will be required to wear a mask upon entering the building. No outside gloves permitted. Gloves will be provided.
  3. Too hot to handle? Upon entering the office, each patient will have their temperature taken, have their hands sanitized, and finally have their oxygen saturation measured with a pulse oximeter. All True Dental team members will be screened each day as well prior to clocking in for the day.
  4. We want YOU to be safe! Each patient will have a health and travel screening.
  5. VIP ONLY! Only the scheduled patient will be allowed into the building. Companions and/or family members will be asked to wait in the car or remain at home.
  6. Hey, we’re hungry too! No outside food or beverages will be allowed in the building.
  7. Less is better. Please limit personal belongings brought into the office.
  8. Is your name on the list? We will not be accepting walk-in patients. All patients will need a scheduled appointment.
  9. Wash away the bacteria. All patients will be receiving an antimicrobial rinse prior to treatment.
  10. The best germ fighting security! We have installed 4 UVC germicidal lights in our HVAC units to purify the air in our building.
  11. The cleanest air around! We have purchased and installed multiple medical grade HEPPA air purifiers throughout the office for additional cleansing of our air.
  12. We’re the Avengers of keeping it clean! Our staff is regularly disinfecting common area surfaces like door handles, rest rooms, and other surfaces.
  13. Nothing gets past us! We also perform daily fogging to clean all surfaces in the office.
